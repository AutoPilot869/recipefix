Chrome extension for home cooks

Inline ingredient badges for recipe directions.
Tap to mark done.

RecipeFix reads the ingredients on any recipe page and adds small tappable badges right inside the directions. As you cook, tap each badge to mark it done and keep your place without losing track.

Visit the RecipeFix homepage

Works on your favorite recipe sites. No account needed.

1
Open any online recipe

Browse to a recipe as you normally would. RecipeFix quietly looks for the ingredient list and the cooking directions on the page.

2
See ingredients as inline badges

When you scroll to the directions, you'll see small badges appear next to each measurement mentioned in a step. Each badge shows the exact quantity from the ingredient list.

3
Tap to mark each one done

As you cook, tap the badges to mark ingredients you've already used. It's a simple visual checklist that lives right where you're reading.
